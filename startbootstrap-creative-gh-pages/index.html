Overview

Plancks Cloud is a distributed, decentralized, cloud computing platform on the blockchain.
Plancks Cloud enables and provides the everyday device owner the ability to rent out their spare compute power and contribute towards a more effective, efficient and shared (?Or distributed?) computing eco-system.

Plancks Cloud also offers affordable solutions for individuals and companies that want to (?utilize spare compute/sell spare compute?) purchase cheaper cloud services. Plancks Cloud is based off open container specifications, which makes it free-market friendly and, for developers and application owners, has the traits of being agnostic. This encourages rapid and easy integration.

Problem

Computation Wastage

In today’s world of technology, processing power is poorly distributed across the world. Even though technology is in some places affordable and cheap, the world still is experiencing a prevalent problem of computational wastage. A wastage where the supply of technological power is not matched with the need for it. An example is where our conventional personal computers have more power then they have needed, and will continue to run without ever safely utilizing 100% of it’s potential.

In addition, the demand for computational power has not waned, it has grown alongside the supply of power however the current avenues and outlets where you can acquire more of this power are in either 1) purchasing better and/or more computer equipment for yourself or 2) renting this power for yourself through a service provider.

Both of these options do not promote effective and efficient use of processing power, it only encourages spending more on the problem, with the chance that the need for this sort of power may be infrequent and/or temporary. Plancks Cloud sees this as a problem that would continue to get worse and worse as technology not only becomes more affordable but also as it advances.

Pricey Cloud Services

The current online cloud industry is growing rapidly, and solutions are constantly on the rise. However despite the growth of cloud, and how technology is increasingly becoming cheaper and more affordable, current services on the cloud and through the cloud are not reflecting these technological changes. Cloud services are still relatively expensive, and not yet casually affordable to everyone. Certain services such as hosting have become cheaper in the last few years, but more advanced cloud services that provide more intensive applications with larger resources are still quite costly, a good example and use case is Amazon Cloud Services and the different services they offer.

In addition to a cloud services rates and charges, the average consumer of these services also has to ensure that they have a predictable budget that won’t be subject to hidden charges, or transitional charges when their service providers implement new policies or changes to their fee structure. Plancks Cloud believes in transparency, stability and accessibility and considers this problem, one worth tackling.

The Solution

A Distributed, Decentralized, Cloud Computation platform

Decentralized &
Scalable

Built from the ground up with P2P architecture, Plancks Cloud will also build in a framework of parallelized computing. Decentralisation and Scalability are fundamental themes in the execution of a true cloud computing platform.

Agnostic &
Simple Integration

Developers will be free to develop in any language and very simply plug it into the open source container technology’s Plancks Cloud is utilizing, which will then plug into Plancks Cloud.

Easy &
Automatic

Suppliers and Users of Plancks Cloud will be able to ask for and select the prices at which they would like the computing power at. This process will be built with the users experience in mind, and will also allow the users the option of automating what compute & how much the compute should sell for. -It Should be as easy as drawing money out the ATM.

Free Market
Centered

By the people, for the people – The Plancks Cloud platform will take into account various computation renting factors (such as - but not limited to: location & intensity), and have it’s prices and markets be completely ruled by the free market. Meaning costs will be transparent predictable, and safe from middlemen intervention.

Community
guided behavior

Plancks Cloud understands that there may be users of the platform who will be deliberately buying computing power for the sake of misbehaving. The Plancks Cloud platform will exhibit absolute transparency in dealing with misbehaviors, and misbehaviors themselves will be dealt with through a community centric process.

Secure &
Stable

Plancks Cloud uses containerization as its core technology, and because of it, all compute take place in these containers and are completely safe and isolated in & from the host system. Additionally measures have been put in place, if the host system goes down, the process will be safely transported to the next host.
