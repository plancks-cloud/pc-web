Planck's Cloud


Planck's Cloud Vision: Cloud The Compute

The Planck's Cloud vision is to be the computing infrastructure of tomorrow, we at Planck's Cloud foresee that the needs for compute will only rise in the future and we believe that a decentralised infrastructure of compute run by a decentralised network that meets the needs of a decentralised market is the best way forward in providing for future generations of technology and people.

Therefore Planck’s Cloud is a blockchain and cloud powered, supercomputer network. Planck's Cloud is a network that will provide and exchange computing power for everyone and anyone who needs it.

Anyone with a device can earn the Planck’s Token (PLK) by supplying their device components to the decentralized network, and likewise, anyone can also spend Planck’s to use their applications on the devices supplied in the decentralized network. Anyone can effectively ‘Rent’ anyone else’s computing power.

Planck’s Cloud won’t be limited to only computer’s and laptops, any device that has an available internet connection like a fridge, washing machine, cellphone or even a datacentre will be able to be a supplier’s on the network.

A Cloud in all of us

Decentralise the world! Let your idle devices work for you!


Earn PLK for renting out computation


Let your spare and unused devices work for you by signing them up as Planck Workers. Use the Planck's Cloud worker software to get paid for renting out computing power to the Planck's Cloud network.


Exchange PLK for USD, BTC, ETH & More


PLK will be tradeable on a number of exchanges and supported by multiple cryptocurrency wallets, allowing you to easily exchange PLK for other currencies like USD, Btc and Eth.


Reliably 'Rent" compute at hyper competitive prices


Planck's Cloud Clients can tune their application usage strategies to suit their needs, creating a custom balance between computate intensity, reliability, and cost. The Planck's Cloud networks makes it possible for suppliers and clients to tailor their compute for each other's needs.

Rapidly Disrupting Markets


The market for cloud compute is still in it's infancy, and as this industry grows and matures every single industry that uses computers will be affected and some majorly disrupted by the innovations that the cloud compute industry will bring. The Planck's Cloud network wants to allow anyone, anywhere the opportunity to participate and be involved as a compute supplier. And through that, offer the different types of computation as it's own commodity. Which will promote competitive pricing through being network driven and further flourishing disruptive innovation.
