Overview

Planck's Cloud is a distributed, decentralized, cloud computing platform on the blockchain.
Planck's Cloud enables and provides the everyday device owner the ability to rent out their spare compute power and contribute towards a more effective, efficient and distributed computing eco-system.

Planck's Cloud also offers affordable solutions for individuals and companies that want to purchase cloud services. Planck's Cloud is based off open container specifications, which makes it free-market friendly and, for developers and application owners, has the traits of being agnostic. This encourages rapid and easy integration.

The Problem


Computation Wastage

In today’s world of technology, processing power is poorly distributed across the world. Even though technology is, in some places, affordable and cheap; the world still is experiencing a prevalent problem of computational wastage. The supply of compute is not matched with the need for it. For example, our conventional computers have more power than they need, and will continue to run without utilizing 100% of its potential.

The demand for compute power has not waned - it has grown alongside the supply of power. The current avenues and outlets where you can acquire more compute power is to either purchase better and/or more computing equipment for yourself, or renting this power through a service provider.

Neither of these options promote effective and efficient use of processing power. It only encourages spending resources on the problem, with the chance that the need for this sort of power may be infrequent and/or temporary. Planck's Cloud sees this as a problem that will continue to worsen as technology advances.

Pricey Cloud Services

The current online cloud industry is growing rapidly, and solutions are constantly on the rise. However despite the growth of cloud, and how technology is becoming increasingly cheaper and more affordable, current services on the cloud and through the cloud are not reflecting these technological changes. Cloud services are still relatively expensive, and not yet casually affordable to everyone. Certain services such as hosting have become cheaper in the last few years, but more larger resources are still quite costly, a good example and use case is Amazon Cloud Services and the different services they offer.

In addition to a cloud services rates and charges, the average consumer of these services also has to ensure that they have a predictable budget that won’t be subject to hidden charges, or transitional charges when their service providers implement new policies or changes to their fee structure. Planck's Cloud believes in transparency, stability and accessibility and considers this problem, one worth tackling.

The Solution

A Distributed, Decentralized, Cloud Computation platform

Decentralized &
Scalable

Built from the ground up with P2P architecture, Planck's Cloud will also build in a framework of parallelized computing. Decentralisation and Scalability are fundamental themes in the execution of a true cloud computing platform.

Agnostic &
Simple Integration

Developers will be free to develop in any language and very simply submit the container to Planck's Cloud which will then be served by someone's spare compute.

Easy &
Automatic

Suppliers and Users of Planck's Cloud will be able to ask for and select the prices at which they would like the computing power at. This process will be built with the users experience in mind, and will also allow the users the option of automating what compute & how much the compute should sell for. It Should be as easy as drawing money out the ATM.

Free Market
Centered

By the people, for the people – The Planck's Cloud platform will take into account various computation renting factors (such as - but not limited to: location & intensity), and have it’s prices and markets be completely ruled by the free market. Meaning costs will be transparent, predictable, and safe from middlemen intervention.

Community
Guided Behavior

Planck's Cloud understands that there may be users of the platform who will be deliberately buying computing power for the sake of misbehaving. The Planck's Cloud platform will exhibit absolute transparency in dealing with misbehaviors, and misbehaviors themselves will be dealt with through a community centric process.

Secure &
Stable

Planck's Cloud uses containerization as its core technology, and because of it, all compute take place in these containers and are completely safe and isolated in & from the host system. Additionally measures have been put in place, if the host system goes down, the process will be safely transported to the next host.
